Problem:
Even after I replace a piece image with my own uploaded cup image, the app keeps changing it back to the blue mug sample image.

1. Search the codebase for all references to:

- seed image
- demo piece

console.log("Saving piece images:", piece.id, piece.imageUrls)
console.log("Loaded piece images:", piece.id, piece.imageUrls)
